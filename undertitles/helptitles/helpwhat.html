I made this website for people to write articles about what is hapening in the world.
But unlike every news site there is, i didn't want any fake news or biased news.
so whenever you submit an article instead of going directly public, it will be reviewd by a moderation team.
And if you ever see a news that is suspicious or fake or biased, you can report it to the moderation team to get it checked.

Our site was desinged to:
1. stop fake news from spreading
2. To keep only the positive news and avoid negative news that talk mainly about politics and religion exct...
3. To help people know the truth about what is hapening in the world.
